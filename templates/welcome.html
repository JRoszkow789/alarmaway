Wake Me Up!

Get Started Instantly and Free! Enter the phone number you'd like to use for your AlarmAway wake up call. Make sure to enter a real number, we need to send you a verification code!

How does it work?

Once you set your Wake Me Up! alarm, simply sit back and trust that you wont miss your wakeup time, no matter where you are.

At your requested wake up time, the Wake Me Up! service will call you, similar to a traditional wake up call. We understand that this isn't always enough however, and that is where the magic comes into play. If the phone number you have given us is a cell phone, shortly after your wakeup call you will receive a text message confirming that your are indeed awake. A simple response to this message shuts off the alarm, and you can go about your day! No response however, prompts a new wake up call, and so on blah blah blah....

Why Wake Me Up! ??

Waking up can be tough sometimes. The snooze button, or worse yet the dreaded accidental push of the OFF button, can ruin a morning, day, or sometimes worse!

We created Wake Me Up! to solve this common problem. Wake Me Up! strives to help keep you on a consistent schedule no matter where you are. Check it out today, and simplify your morning.
